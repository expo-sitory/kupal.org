Kupal Mc | Civilization Series

Welcome to Season 1

Embark on a journey of nation-building across a meticulously crafted 1:40 scale replica of the Philippine archipelago. This is not just Minecraft, this is civilization at scale, where your choices shape the fate of empires.

Core Concept

Build, trade, and thrive as you establish your civilization on one of three major islands:

  • Luzon
  • Visayas
  • Mindanao

Twist: You choose your island, but your ethnicity is assigned randomly upon join. Adapt or perish.

Season 1 Features

[ Ethnicity System ]

Your ethnicity isn't just flavor! it's mechanical.

  • Unique Crafting Recipes – Each ethnicity unlocks exclusive recipes for nether and end items, forcing specialization and trade
  • Cultural Traits – Unique buffs and debuffs shape your gameplay:
    • Some ethnicities excel at mining and agriculture
    • Others master trade, magic, or warfare
[ Survival Mechanics ]
  • Nether & End Disabled – Traditional exploration banned
  • Ethnicity-Locked Progression – You must trade with other players to access forbidden resources
  • Mandatory Interaction – Solo play won't work; collaboration is survival
[ Economy & Trading ]

There is no NPC marketplace. No admin shops. Only player-driven economy

  • Establish trade routes between islands
  • Negotiate prices and treaties
  • Control rare resources through your ethnicity's monopoly
  • Build wealth through commerce, not grinding
[ Play Style ]
  • For players who want strategy and roleplay
  • For communities that love political intrigue and negotiation
  • For those tired of vanilla Minecraft
  • For builders with a purpose, create to expand your civilization
Ready to Build an Empire?

Join Season 1 and write the history of the Philippine Archipelago. Will your civilization rise, or will it crumble under the weight of resource scarcity and rival nations?
